About Fin's Limited Store

  • Reliable Service
  • Affordable Prices
  • Everyday Convenience

Fin's Limited Store was established in June 2024 with a clear mission to provide the students of Oyo State College of Technology{OYSCATECH} with premium essential services. Founded and led by our CEO, Nasiru Fridaous Oladimeji (born in year 2002), the company was born out of a need to solve the persistent electricity challenges in Pako Tuntun community of Igbo-Ora, OYO.

Fin’s Limited Store is a neighborhood convenience and service store dedicated to providing refreshing beverages, reliable phone charging services, and enjoyable gaming entertainment in a comfortable and customer-friendly environment. Our goal is to make everyday services simple, fast, and accessible for students, workers, and residents in our community. We focus on delivering quality service with convenience and customer satisfaction as our top priority.

What We Offer

  • Cold soft drinks and refreshing beverages 🥤
  • Fast and reliable phone charging services 📱
  • Power bank charging support 🔋
  • Comfortable gaming entertainment sessions 🎮

Our services are designed to give customers a convenient place to relax, recharge their devices, and enjoy quality refreshments anytime during the day.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide dependable everyday services at affordable prices while creating a welcoming space where customers feel comfortable and valued. We are committed to maintaining reliable service standards and ensuring every visit to Fin’s Limited Store is simple, fast, and enjoyable.
